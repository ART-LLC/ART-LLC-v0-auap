# AUAPW LLC - Compliance & Banking Documentation

## Company Information

**Business Name:** AUAPW LLC (America's Used Auto Parts Warehouse)  
**Business Type:** E-commerce Marketplace for Used Auto Parts  
**Founded:** 2024  
**Headquarters:** United States  
**Primary Service:** Used OEM auto parts marketplace connecting customers with 2,000+ verified suppliers nationwide  

## Data Protection & Privacy

### Personal Data Handling
- Customer data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
- Personal information is only used for order processing, customer support, and marketing (with consent)
- Data retention follows industry best practices (3-7 years for transaction records)
- GDPR and CCPA compliant data handling procedures
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

### Customer Information Security
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
- Session tokens are securely managed with HTTPOnly, Secure, and SameSite flags
- API endpoints require authentication for sensitive operations
- All customer data is encrypted at rest using AES-256

## Payment Processing

### Payment Security Standards
- PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant infrastructure
- Never store raw credit card data - payment processing delegated to Stripe or equivalent processors
- Immediate encryption of payment information
- Fraud detection and prevention systems in place

### Payment Methods Supported
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Bank transfers for B2B customers

## Order & Transaction Management

### Order Fulfillment
- All orders are tracked and documented
- Transaction records maintained for 7 years minimum
- Automated order confirmations and shipping notifications
- Clear refund and return policies communicated pre-purchase

### Financial Reconciliation
- Daily reconciliation of payments received vs. orders processed
- Monthly financial reporting and audits
- Tax-compliant invoice generation for all transactions
- Escrow system for marketplace transactions (supplier-to-customer)

## Warranty & Consumer Protection

### Part Warranties
- 30-180 day warranties on all eligible parts
- Clear warranty terms provided at purchase
- Hassle-free warranty claim process
- Partial refunds or replacements as per terms

### Consumer Rights
- Full compliance with FTC regulations
- Clear terms and conditions available to all customers
- Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
- 24-hour customer support for inquiries and issues

## KYC & AML Compliance

### Know Your Customer (KYC)
- Verified email and address collection
- For B2B customers: Business verification and tax ID collection
- Age verification for certain restricted products
- No service to customers on OFAC sanctions lists

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)
- Transaction monitoring for suspicious activity
- Reporting thresholds aligned with regulations
- Customer verification before large transactions
- Compliance with FinCEN regulations
